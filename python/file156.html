Q&A with ClicData on business intelligence tools and the future of business analytics

By Community Team July 25th, 2019

—

Below is a Q&A session with Telmo Silva, CEO at ClicData. Telmo is a University of Toronto Engineering graduate (BASc) has circumnavigated the globe implementing ERP, CRM, and BI enterprise applications in food/retail, pharmaceuticals and healthcare, and manufacturing. 

He has contributed to several papers on Business Intelligence and Artificial Intelligence or Machine Learning. He’s also a contributor to the “Bioinformatics and Computational Biology in Drug Discovery and Development” for the Cambridge University Press with a section entitled “Information Visualization-important IT considerations”.

Telmo Silva, CEO, ClicData

Q: First and foremost, can you please share with us a brief overview of your company? When was ClicData established and what are your goals?

ClicData Infinis was first launched in 2010, since then we launched 4 versions of the platform now called ClicData i4. ClicData is a leading Business Intelligence vendor as we offer a fully cloud-based data warehouse and automated ETL features in addition to data visualization. 

We wanted ClicData to be a flexible but powerful alternative to other solutions on the market which were exclusively on-premise and expensive to maintain software. Besides, none of them covered the whole process of data analytics: data connection, data storage, and management in a data warehouse, schedules and alerts automation, data visualization and dashboard sharing capabilities. So we did. 

The first 3 versions of ClicData were the culmination of years of research and on how to deliver high-quality online data analytics tools to anyone in a matter of seconds.

Today, we are a 100%-cloud and automated Business Intelligence platform. We host over 10TB of data and publish over 30,000 dashboards on a daily basis.

Q: What industries do you serve and who are your current customers?

ClicData can serve any company or organization that needs to analyze data to make smarter decisions. So, every company in every industry. We have customers in multiple verticals such as public services, education, healthcare, food industry, finance, retail…

We work with US Mortgage Corporation, Siemens, Naphcare, Lexmark… And hundreds of SMBs on all continents. We can also distinguish 3 types of users within these companies: 

  • Business Managers: usually working in Marketing, Sales, Accounting departments
  • Data Professionals: CTOs, CDOs, Data Analysts 
  • Business Consultants within Consulting Firms or Freelancers

With a single platform, we’re able to cater to any business needs and data types.

Q: How is ClicData different from other BI applications?

ClicData is different from other Business Intelligence tools on many levels.

First of all, many of the most popular tools on the market are actually data visualization tools. There’s a difference between BI, data visualization and data analytics. ClicData is a true Business Intelligence tool as we include a data warehouse where you can centralize and store up to 8TB of data from 250+ sources. We also offer ETL features that enable you to automatically clean, merge, combine, transform or standardize data from multiple datasets. Basically, we automate what you spend hours trying to do in Excel.

BI without ClicData

BI with ClicData

We are and always have been a 100% cloud-based SaaS platform, in fact, ClicData was the world’s first, 100% cloud-based BI solution. Once you’ve signed up, you can immediately start playing with the application and building your first dashboard. We also designed the UI so that non-technical users can play around and get things done without relying on IT. 

We help you break the silos! There’s no point of gathering and analyzing data if you don’t share insights with your teams or customers. That’s why we allow you to share your dynamic, real-time dashboards via simple LiveLinks working on every browser and any device. 

ClicData’s licensing if extremely flexible allowing for easy use by teams and departments. Additionally, you can share your dashboards with anyone, anywhere, even those without a license using our LiveLinks.

Here’s an interesting use case of why ClicData has been chosen by this company over Power BI and Tableau.

Q: According to Forbes, 2018 demonstrated that dynamic dashboards, reporting, end-user self-service, advanced visualization, and data warehousing are the top five most important technologies that are critical to Business Intelligence. As experts in Business Intelligence and Data Warehousing tools, how does ClicData empower everyone within organizations?

What’s interesting in The State of Business Intelligence in 2018 published on Forbes, is that end-user self-service is the third-most-important expected initiative in the Business Intelligence industry. Why? Organizations need to be more agile and flexible with their data analytics processes and enable their employees to get reports whenever they need. Unfortunately, it’s still not the case for the majority. Many departments are still relying on IT to extract data from different sources like applications or databases, after what they spend hours cleansing and combining data in Excel spreadsheets. That causes unnecessary stress and frustration for end-users and it’s a huge waste of their time and not to mention, extremely error-prone.

Offering real-time and effective KPI reporting ensures that the team, department, and company are all working from a single version of the truth. This is why the Self-Service BI is important

Q: Tell us more about ClicData i4. What are its key features and capabilities? 

As mentioned above, ClicData i4 is a powerful engine that enables any kind of data processing and analysis. 

First, you’re able to extract only the data you need for your reporting. For instance, you certainly don’t need every column of every table in your database to build your sales dashboard for the European market. When connecting ClicData to your application or database, you’re given the choice of metrics you want to compare or calculate, ClicData will automatically create your new dataset. 

ClicData is also a place to centralize, combine and clean your data. 

  • Our data warehouse enables much faster access to your data
  • It ensures data consistency by defining new relationships between your datasets
  • Keep your data flowing when you’re upgrading or migrating from an application to another
  • Keep track of historical data if you need to compare or restore data

You can also customize the time of your data refresh. It could be every Monday at 8:00am so that your dashboards and KPIs are up to date when you get in, ready to be monitored and analyzed while sipping your morning coffee. You can define different parameters on every dataset.

Q: Can you provide us with sample use cases for ClicData?

Read how one company went from days to seconds to produce complex customer reporting that they now deliver on an “any-time” basis to every client (internal and external)

Q: Looking into the future, what are some market trends or technologies you see on the rise in the BI space and how is ClicData meeting these?

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning are progressively integrated into all kinds of tools and technologies in both B2C and B2B markets. Business Intelligence tools are no exception. 

As mentioned before, BI tools are more and more used by non-technical end-users, this means that we need to design intuitive UIs and make the user experience as smooth as possible. That’s what we did with ClicData. But BI is still a complex task to achieve because it requires calculations, data combination and manipulation that most of them don’t know about. 

Besides, Business Intelligence focuses on the analysis of historical data and asking ourselves “why this happened” which is really important to improve current actions. But it doesn’t answer predictive questions such as “how many of this product can we expect to sell in 6 months?”.

Data Science and technologies like AI and ML would be used to overcome both these challenges.

ClicData integrates with existing automated predictive analytics platforms such as Prevision.IO or H2O.ai. We also allow you to implement custom AI algorithms using R or Python. 

About ClicData

ClicData is the world first 100% cloud-based Business Intelligence and data warehousing platform that connects with any data types from any sources. We’re also 100% SaaS, no installation needed, your account is up and running within seconds. With a single platform, you can centralize, clean, combine, transform and visualize your data to build meaningful dashboards. Create dynamic and real-time reports that you can share with anybody within your company or with customers via our LiveLinks compatible on every browser and every device.
